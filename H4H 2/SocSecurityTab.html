Social Security Card

A Social Security Card can be obtained in person. If a person is requesting a replacement social security card (because they have a number), then they can request it by mail [or online, if a person has a state issued ID/DL and is not changing their name]

Social Security Office Locator
People must show proof of age (usually birth certificate) + proof of ID (usually DL or state ID, they do not currently accept CDCR ID). Without a photo ID, people can submit the following documents to try to prove their identities (they must be originals, which will be returned):
  • MediCal card
  • U.S. military ID
  • Certificate of Naturalization
  • Health insurance card
  • Certified copy of medical record
  • Student ID/record (if a person does not have a photo ID, going in person may be a better idea for providing identity that mailing in documents)
  • Tip: You can get temporary MediCal card in 24 hours by calling Department of Social Services if card has not arrived
Application for a Social Security Card: Social Security Administration Form

Note: Fill out name change portion of form and prove name change to get different name on social security card

There are no fees.
